What is "Fika" time

This is typically Swedish and simply means taking a break from work and duties to have a coffee or other snack in the company of friends, family or friends. Usually, as a snack during fika, Swedes eat "kannellbulle" - cinnamon rolls, often with the addition of cardamom. You can get them everywhere, just like our donuts.

Swedish holiday magic

Advent and the day of Santa Lucia

The Advent season in Sweden is definitely different from ours. It begins with the feast of Saint Lucy on December 13 and as the shortest day of the year, it is a symbol of the triumph of light over the darkness - from then on, especially in the North, lights will be coming. There are parades all over town. women walk in white in the church, special songs are sung, headed by "Lucia" - a girl who wears a candle crown that day.

Swedish Sweets Day

Semlor

On the last day before the fast, the Swedes eat another delicacy - Semlor, which is marzipan buns and whipped cream. Traditionally, Swedes eat it with milk - some drink it in a glass and others ... put semla in a deep plate, sprinkle cinnamon around and then pour warm milk until the Semla is soft.
